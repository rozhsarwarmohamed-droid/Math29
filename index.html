💡 Key Idea
📐
Finding a fraction is just like division!
Sort the whole into equal groups to find the fraction.

Example: ⅕ of 60 = 60 ÷ 5 = 12
🔑
The denominator (bottom number) tells you how many equal parts to divide into.
The numerator (top number) tells you how many parts you take.
📖 Key Vocabulary
denominator numerator unit fraction non-unit fraction diagram dividing line
💬
Unit fraction = numerator is 1 (like ½, ⅓, ¼)
Non-unit fraction = numerator is more than 1 (like ¾, ²⁄₅)
